# Fortuna Avatar — Image Generation Prompt

## Tool: Nano Banana

## Prompt

A sleek, minimalist avatar icon of "Fortuna", a sophisticated AI financial advisor. Female-coded abstract figure or stylized face with an air of confidence and luxury. The design evokes private banking and wealth management — elegant, authoritative, and modern.

Color palette: deep navy blue (#1a1a3e) as the base, with accents of radiant gold and teal/emerald highlights. The gold should feel like a subtle glow, not flashy — think understated luxury. Background should be transparent or very dark navy.

Style: clean vector-like illustration, geometric and minimal. No photorealism. Think fintech app icon meets high-end brand mark. Rounded silhouette that works well as a circular avatar at small sizes (40-64px). The figure should convey intelligence, precision, and trustworthiness.

Mood: sophisticated, calm, opinionated, premium. Like a personal wealth advisor at an exclusive private bank. No generic robot or chatbot aesthetic — this is a refined financial intelligence, not a cute assistant.

The gradient should flow from navy (hue 250) through teal (hue 165) to gold (hue 75), matching the app's signature AI gradient. Subtle mesh gradient depth is welcome.

## Style Modifiers

Adjust depending on what Nano Banana responds best to:

- `flat design`, `app icon`, `3D render`
- `vector art`, `geometric`, `abstract portrait`
- `dark background`, `circular crop`, `icon design`

## Brand Reference

- **Navy:** oklch(0.10 0.035 250) — deep, dramatic
- **Teal:** oklch(0.55 0.18 165) — vibrant accent
- **Gold:** oklch(0.62 0.18 75) — radiant warmth
- **AI Gradient:** 135deg from navy → teal → gold
